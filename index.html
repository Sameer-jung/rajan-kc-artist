Rajan KC is a Kathmandu-based rapper, poet, and songwriter whose music refuses comfort. Rooted in Nepali soil and shaped by lived contradiction, his verses reject spectacle — choosing instead the raw architecture of honesty.

From the debut album MA (2022) through a series of incendiary 2025 singles, his catalog maps the interior of a generation confronting identity, injustice, and the bittersweet weight of home. NepHop's uncompromising voice.
